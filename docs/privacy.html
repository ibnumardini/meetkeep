Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-24

MeetKeep does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information, financial records, authentication details, or meeting contents.

The extension collects only a single anonymous daily ping to count active users. This ping contains no personal data and no meeting content, it's used solely to tell whether the extension is actually being used.

You can opt out of this ping at any time via the extension popup settings (click the MeetKeep icon in your toolbar and switch off Send ping analytics). It's on by default, and turning it off takes effect immediately.

Questions about this policy? Contact hi@mardini.dev.
